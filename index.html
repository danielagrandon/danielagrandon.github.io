About me

I'm a cosmologist working at the intersection of theoretical cosmology and data science. I am a postdoctoral research associate and lecturer at Leiden University, The Netherlands. Broadly speaking, my research focuses on studying non-Gaussianities in cosmic fields and performing accurate, precise statistical analyses to infer cosmological parameters. I'm particularly interested in cosmological probes such as weak gravitational lensing and in developing statistical methods based on machine learning and Bayesian inference. I'm an active member of the Rubin LSST and Euclid collaborations. I co-lead the Higher Order Statistics Topical Team within LSST-DESC, and co-lead of the Data Release 1 Euclid cosmological analysis using higher-order statistics. In 2022, I was awarded the L'Oréal-UNESCO "For Women in Science" Prize in Chile.
